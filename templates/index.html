International Helplines

  • US citizens: +1-888-407-4747 (State Department)
  • Spain: 119 Spanish nationals reported missing — consular emergency line active
  • Google Person Finder: google.org/personfinder

Safety

⚠️ Aftershock risk: 43% chance of Mw 6+ within the next week. Do not enter visibly damaged structures. Avoid hillside areas (landslide risk). Monitor official advisories.

What to Do

  • Drop, Cover, Hold On during aftershocks
  • Stay away from damaged buildings, overpasses, and slopes
  • Check on neighbours — especially elderly and children
  • Use text messages instead of calls to reduce network congestion
  • Charge devices now — widespread power outages reported
  • Drink bottled or boiled water until supply is confirmed safe
